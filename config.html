Data collection & Trading

Modify the resources/secrets.json file, and add your twitter and reddit API keys. This will enable you to programmatically access the twitter and Reddit APIs. Also add your gdax key, secret, and pass phrase for automated trading. Goes without saying that you should be very careful with these, and only start live trading when you are sure you won't be losing money.
